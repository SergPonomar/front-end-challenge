Library

This is a collection of additional resources that might be useful in building a Hygge company.

Handbooks

  • Old Valve Handbook heavily influenced our guiding principles. Valve is a great example of a company that has been pumping out cutting-edge products for decades without becoming a cancerous evil corp.
  • GitLab with its Remote Playbook and Handbook is another place where we stole ideas from.
  • Zapier has a lot of materials about remote work and great tips about internal communication in distributed teams.
  • Buffer shares everything they know about remote work in their blog, and some ideas resonate with tne Hygge work approach.
